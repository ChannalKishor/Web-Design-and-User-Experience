The Yoga capital of the world and the gateway to Garhwal Himalayas, Rishikesh is one of the most sought-after destinations by travel and adventure enthusiasts from all across the globe. Rishikesh is located at the bottom of the great Himalayas. If you are someone who was looking for a trip that is soaked in the ideal hues of fun and adventure then a trip to Rishikesh in summers is the ideal choice for you. Rishikesh is a major pilgrimage spot for Hindus the holy river Ganga flows through this place and it is the home of various yoga ashrams and shrines. However, for adventure enthusiasts, Rishikesh is a great hub for all the adventure activities. One can go river rafting and even bungee jumping. For a thrilling experience, camping is one of the best things to do in Rishikesh in summer. The place offers something for every kind of traveler and hence Rishikesh is a great holiday spot for all kinds of travelers in the summer season.
